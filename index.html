Migrant Entrepreneurs: Creating our jobs.

The Centre for Entrepreneurs and DueDil have partnered to conduct a comprehensive, first of a kind, analysis of the contribution that migrant entrepreneurs make to the UK.

The Facts

480,000

New Businesses

Over the last 20 years, four hundred and eighty thousand migrants have been responsible for founding over four hundred and eighty thousand new businesses.

New Jobs

New Growth
